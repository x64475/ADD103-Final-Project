Respawn Lounge

Support

Welcome to the Respawn Lounge Support Center! We are here to make sure your gaming experience is smooth, fun, and frustration-free. If you run into any issues, you will find help below.

Common Issues

Account & Membership

Can not log in? Double-check your username and password. If you forgot your password, use the “Reset Password” option on the login screen.

Membership not working? Make sure your subscription is active. If you recently renewed, log out and back in to refresh your access.

Equipment & Gameplay

Game not launching? Try restarting the system or selecting a different station. If the issue continues, notify staff.

Lag or performance issues? This may be due to high traffic. Switching stations or lowering in-game settings can help.

Controller or headset not working? Check connections first. If it still does not work, request a replacement at the front desk.

Payments & Bookings

Charged incorrectly? Contact us with your receipt or booking ID, and we will review it as soon as possible.

Reservation issues? Make sure your booking time has not expired. Late arrivals may result in cancellation during peak hours.

House Rules & Safety

To keep Respawn Lounge enjoyable for everyone:

Report damaged equipment immediately

Food and drinks only in designated areas

No cheating, hacking, or exploiting systems

Respect other players and staff


Failure to follow these rules may result in suspension or removal.
